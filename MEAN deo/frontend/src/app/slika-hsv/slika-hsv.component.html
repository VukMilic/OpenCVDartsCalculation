CALCULATE DART POINTS APP

Welcome to the game of darts!
Before entering a game, there are a few requirements that need to be followed in order for this application to work. This application is based on color recognitions. The colors of the official dartboard are RED, GREEN, BLACK and WHITE. The colors that are used for calculations are RED and GREEN. So, if you want calculations to be precise, you need to have the darts with the different color than those two (suggestions: BLUE, YELLOW...)
- OFFICIAL DARTBOARD:
REQUIREMENTS:
  • You need to take your dartboard photos as near to the board as you can (all fields of the dartboard must be visible).
  • It would be ideal to cover up any red or green items that are in the area around the dartboard that will appear in the photo with a different hue.
  • You need to color your darts on two sides:
    • Top of the FLIGHT part (any color besides dartboard colors)
    • BARREL and TIP (any color besides dartboard colors and flight color)
  • In order for app to know what color you are using on the flight top and barrel/tip, you will need to upload three different photos of the darts on your dartboard.
